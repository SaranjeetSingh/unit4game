Crystal collector!!!


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game matching your total score with random number. You lose the game by exceeding your number from random number.

The value of crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game will start the game will change the value of each crystal.
